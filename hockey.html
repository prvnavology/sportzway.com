Tips For Your Fantasy Squad

Know Your Scoring System +

Understand how your league scores points. Whether it's standard scoring, PPR (points per reception), or another format, tailor your draft and lineup decisions accordingly.

Do Your Research +

Stay updated with player news, injuries, and depth chart changes. Follow expert analyses and use reliable sources to make informed decisions.

Mock Drafts+

Practice makes perfect. Participate in mock drafts to familiarize yourself with different draft strategies, player values, and draft positions.

Balance Risk and Consistency +

While high-risk players can provide upside, balance them with consistent performers who deliver reliable points week after week.

Manage Your Roster +

Stay active throughout the season. Monitor waiver wires for breakout players and potential replacements for injured or underperforming players on your roster.

Stay Flexible +

Be willing to make adjustments. Adapt your lineup based on matchups, player form, and injuries as the season progresses.

Understand Bye Weeks +

Plan ahead for bye weeks to ensure you have backups or replacements for players who will be unavailable during those weeks.

Don't Overreact +

Avoid making knee-jerk reactions after one bad game or a slow start. Stay patient with your draft picks and trust your initial evaluations.

Use Trades Wisely +

Evaluate trade offers carefully. Consider how a trade will impact your team's depth, positional strength, and overall scoring potential before accepting or rejecting.

Enjoy the Game +

Fantasy football is about having fun and engaging with the sport you love. Stay engaged with your league mates, enjoy the competition, and celebrate your successes, big or small.
